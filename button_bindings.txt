RB: Grab game piece
LB: Release game piece

A: Position arm at low (preset)
B: Position arm at mid (preset)
Y: Position arm at high (preset)
X: Toggle extending/retracting intake

D-pad up: Slightly raise arm position
D-pad down: Slightly lower arm position
D-pad left: Lower drivetrain speed
D-pad right: Raise drivetrain speed
